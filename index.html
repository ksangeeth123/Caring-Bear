Welcome to Care-a-lot!

This is an immersive interactive web experience where your care can brighten up a sad, sulking bear.

Hold to Care

Press and hold your mouse or touch the bear to start the Care Bear Stare. The rotating rainbow backdrop will spin, and the bear will glow in colorful joy!

Keyboard Shortcuts

You can hold down the Spacebar key at any time to activate the Care Bear Stare beam.

Customization Workshop

Use the panels to switch between presets like Grumpy Bear or Cheer Bear, slide colors to paint your own bear, and control the weather system!

Magical Synthesizer

Enable sound to listen to the Web Audio ambient synthesizers. You will hear raindrops falling and musical chimes sparkling as you interact.
